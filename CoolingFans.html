COOLING FANS

Cooling fans, such as those used in electronic devices or as standalone fans, typically consist of several components. The specific parts may vary depending on the type and design of the fan.


What do they consist of?


  1. . Plastic Housing: The outer casing of the fan.
  2. Metal Blades: The rotating blades responsible for airflow.
  3. Motor: The motor that drives the fan.
  4. Power Cord:The cord that provides electrical power.
  5. Packaging Materials: Materials like cardboard or plastic used in packaging.
